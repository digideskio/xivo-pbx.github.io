New Asterisk 13 training in France

Posted on Tue 15 September 2015 in articles

Hello,

XiVO is a big platform for Unified Communication and our team is actively improving it, especially by using lot of external great components like Asterisk. So if you're interested in understanding one of the major components in Asterisk, Avencall now offers training on Asterisk 13 in France. Furthermore, you will be in direct contact with the Avencall team and you can ask about the interaction between Asterisk and XiVO.

Link in English : http://www.avencall.com/en/new-asterisk-13-training-in-france/

Link in French : http://www.avencall.com/nouvelle-formation-asterisk-13-france/

Happy XiVO and Asterisk!
